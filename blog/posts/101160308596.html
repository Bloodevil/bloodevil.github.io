 if you want to use wifi on mobile, just you can click the “Korean” button located in right top. then you can see korean page. then you click the all “약관 동의” and “이름 name” “메일 e-mail” (no matter right or wrong. you don’t need right name and e-mail) after enter the input box, you can see another box looks like phone number input box. then just you write number start with 010 (no matter the other just fill the box like 01012345678)

 then you can use the olleh_starbucks wifi. :)
